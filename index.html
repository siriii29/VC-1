Breast Cancer Detection using CNN & RNN

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers among women. I developed a hybrid Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) model to detect stages of breast cancer from mammogram images. This model combines spatial (CNN) and temporal (RNN) features to classify stages like Normal, Beginning, and Malignant. It outperformed traditional models in both accuracy and speed, showing promise in real clinical settings.
